A Few Things I Go By

The short version
i.

Most things are systems. Learn to read one and the rest get a lot easier.

ii.

Complexity is fine — confusion isn't. I really enjoy making tangled things make sense.

iii.

To understand something, start with what it's connected to.

iv.

I'd rather learn a lot of things than get too precious about just one.

v.

Stay curious. The day I think I've got it all figured out is the day I've stopped learning.

vi.

Just build the thing. A rough version that works beats a perfect one that doesn't exist.
